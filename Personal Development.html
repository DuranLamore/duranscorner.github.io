PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

MY STRENGTHS

In my opinion, 3 strengths that I possess are problem solving skills, critical thinking and confidence.
When talking about my problem solving skills, I would say that they have been developed through difficult situations over the years
whether it was academical or in my personal life. Through these obstacles I have garnered a respectable skillset in those regards.
My critical on the otherhand wasn't always something I possesed growing up. As stated before I was in enough problems to be solving them,
but not always in the best ways. Through my envolvement in sports and having a good grasp on most of my subjects I learned to hone my critical thinking
which still need improvement and will be improved in the coming years. Finally, I haven't always been a confident person as I wasn't as secure
of myself as I am now. It was through peer encouragement, self reflection and determination that has given me the standing I have today.

SUMMARY OF TWELVE PILLARS:A novel Chapters 4-7


In my understanding, the text is trying to instill that goal setting is deciding where you want to go and then designing a plan to get you there.
Setting goals that stretch you and make you do things differently is what will lead you to a future that you want.
Don’t wait for people to set goals for you. We all must suffer from one of two pains: the pain of discipline or the pain of regret.
The difference is that discipline weighs ounces and regret weights tons.
Your best opportunity each day are those that align with your overall goals and should be what you spend your time on.
Don’t just go through the day, get from the day. Don’t spend you time on urgent, spend your time on what is important.
We must be our own designer of our lives.

of setting goals and wanting to keep striving towards them, I carry the weight of my destiny in my hands and so taking on challenges should be rewarding
in the aspect of earning what I long for.
